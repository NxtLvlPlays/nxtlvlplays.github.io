Til' Death       Do Us Part

Til' Death Do Us Part is a small RPG game developed in RPGMaker MV. It was developed in 16 weeks for Webster University's Digital RPG Design course and was a first attempted at game design.

Become a highschool student and solve the mysteries around Pin High! Who is trapping you and your friends overnight, and why other students have disappeared? Try and survive the night with your friends in tact and uncover the belly of the beast. Check out the game on Itch!
